## 1. Implement Sub-Sample Bit Timing
To prevent "Frame Drift" (which causes the Rolfe Christmas Tree bug), do not round bit samples. Use an accumulator to track the exact timing of the 4800 Baud clock.
- **Samples per bit:** 9.1875
- **Goal:** Ensure the TDM frame (96 bits) ends exactly at sample 882 (44100 / 50Hz).

## 2. Implement "High-Frequency Screech" (BMC Logic)
Update the `generateBMCSignal` function:
- **Every Bit:** Toggle the signal at the start of the bit period.
- **If Bit is '1':** Toggle the signal AGAIN exactly halfway through the bit period (at sample 4.5).
- **If Bit is '0':** Do NOT toggle in the middle.
- **Waveform:** Use a hard `1.0` and `-1.0` (Square Wave).
